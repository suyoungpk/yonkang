공연장 운영

  • Yonkang Hall
    Yonkang Hall was established in the birthplace of the late Yonkang(蓮崗) Too Pyung Park, the founder of Doosan Group, in 1993 as part of the project to commemorate the 100th anniversary of Doosan Group. It was remodeled as a professional venue for performances with 620 seats under a renovation project in 2007.

  • Space 111
    Space 111 was opened to commemorate the 111th anniversary of Doosan Group’s establishment. It is designed as a space in which Doosan-sponsored artists can experiment with their art, and produce and perform new works.

갤러리 운영

  • Doosan Gallery Seoul
    Doosan Gallery Seoul supports emerging Korean artists by exhibiting their works, presenting the current state of Korean contemporary art to an international audience. In addition, Doosan Gallery Seoul offers diverse programs to expand the horizon of Korean contemporary art, including Doosan Curator Workshop, which fosters emerging curators, and Doosan Art School, which offers a series of lectures open to the general public.

  • Doosan Gallery New York
    Doosan Gallery New York was founded in 2009 in Chelsea, New York City as the first Korean non-profit organization to be recognized by both the State of New York and New York Department of Education. Doosan Gallery New York provides an opportunity for a solo exhibition for emerging Korean artists based throughout the world and supports them to continue their practices internationally.

Education

  • Doosan Humanities Theater
    Doosan Humanities Theater is a creative venue where sociological, humanistic, and artistic imaginations meet.Each year, performances, exhibitions, lectures, and film screenings that follow a specially selected theme are held at Doosan Arts Center.

  • Doosan Art School
    Doosan Art School provides free lectures designed to help the general public become more familiar with and gain a greater appreciation of contemporary art at home and abroad. Doosan Art School for Children provides education programs for local children with little experience of culture and arts. Integrated arts education based on storytelling helps children to familiarize themselves with art and nurture their creative talent.

  • Backstage Tour
    Backstage Tour offers a learning experience of Doosan ART CENTER’s history, facilities and major programs while exploring Yonkang Hall, Space111, Doosan Gallery Seoul, and other facilities.
